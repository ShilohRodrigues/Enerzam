About Us

At EnerZam, we're passionate about protecting our planet and empowering businesses to combat climate change through Greenhouse Gas (GHG) Reduction, Net Zero Initiatives and Decarbonization Strategies.

Established in 2015, EnerZam is headquartered in the historic Old Port of Montreal. In 2019, we expanded our reach into the Ontario market with the opening of our Toronto office. What sets EnerZam apart is our unique integration of two distinct disciplines under one roof.

Our team of Energy Efficiency & Building Engineers collaborates closely with clients on real-world projects, while our Technology Division, comprising software and hardware engineers as well as data scientists, works in tandem with our field engineers. Together, they develop and deploy cutting-edge tools designed to achieve ambitious energy efficiency and reduction targets.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower you to embark on a journey toward sustainability by embracing energy-efficient engineering solutions and science-based targets. These initiatives not only reduce your carbon footprint but also enhance your building's performance, leading to substantial cost savings.

By incorporating advanced smart technologies and implementing expert energy conservation strategies, we will help you unlock the full potential of your building. Together, we will chart a course toward a greener future while ensuring compliance with the latest government environmental regulations.

Our Vision

At EnerZam, we seamlessly integrate building engineering expertise with cutting-edge software development to deliver innovative, industry-leading solutions. Our commitment to excellence and continuous improvement has led to the establishment of a dedicated technology division, specializing in intelligent and adaptive software systems.

Our modular and scalable approach allows us to tailor bespoke software solutions that precisely address each client's unique requirements. By leveraging our passion for innovation and unwavering dedication to quality, we empower organizations to streamline their operations and achieve ambitious energy reduction goals. With EnerZam as your partner, you'll unlock new levels of efficiency and sustainability in your building management processes.
