Hospitals

Hospitals operate around the clock, with high demands for electricity and thermal energy every day of the year. They require stringent ventilation, air conditioning, and air quality controls to ensure a safe and effective environment for patients. According to the Canada Green Building Council, "a large percentage of Canada's hospital buildings were constructed before 1990, when energy efficiency standards and green building practices were less common." This highlights a significant opportunity for improvement, as hospitals exhibit the highest energy consumption per unit of floor area among public-use buildings, leading to substantial utility expenses.

Understanding the critical nature of healthcare services, EnerZam offers comprehensive solutions tailored to enhance energy efficiency and sustainability in your facility.
